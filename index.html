AI Job-Recommender

AI Job Recommender is a smart job-matching system that simplifies the job search process by using AI to scan and analyze user resumes. The system automatically extracts key skills, qualifications, and experience from the uploaded resume using Natural Language Processing (NLP). Based on the extracted information, it matches the user's profile with relevant job listings from a wide database. The AI engine ensures that the recommendations are not just keyword-based but contextually accurate to the user’s strengths. Users receive a curated list of job opportunities that align with their real abilities and career interests. The system eliminates the need for manually entering skills, making the process fast and effortless. It also adapts over time by learning from user preferences and feedback. Recruiters benefit by receiving better-matched candidates with verified skill relevance. The platform offers a user-friendly experience for both job seekers and recruiters. Overall, it creates a seamless bridge between talent and opportunity using the power of intelligent automation.

GIPHY clone
